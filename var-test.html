var() font test

  • var(--dejavusans) = works (DejaVu)
  • var(--freesans) = works (Free Sans)
  • var(--notosans) = works (Noto)
  • var(--notosans), 'freesans-test-one' = works (Noto)
  • var(--notosans2), 'freesans-test-one' = fails, nothing outside of var()
  • var(--notosans, 'freesans-test-one') = works (Noto)
  • var(--notosans2, 'freesans-test-one') = works (Free Sans)
  • var(--notosans, 'freesans-test-one', 'dejavu-sans-test-one') = works (Noto)
  • var(--notosans2, 'freesans-test-one', 'dejavu-sans-test-one') = works (Free Sans)
  • var(--notosans2, 'freesans-test-two', 'dejavu-sans-test-one')
    • Firefox: = fails, should be 2nd fallback
    • Chromium (Edge, Whale): = works (DejaVu)
  • var(--notosans2, var(--freesans), 'dejavu-sans-test-one') = works (Free Sans), because 1st fallback is valid
  • var(--notosans2, var(--freesans2), 'dejavu-sans-test-one') = fails, nothing outside of var()
  • var(--notosans2, 'dejavu-sans-test-one', var(--freesans)) = fails, nothing outside of var()
  • var(--notosans2, 'dejavu-sans-test-one', var(--freesans2)) = fails, nothing outside of var()
  • var(--notosans2, 'dejavu-sans-test-two', var(--freesans))
    • Firefox: = fails, nothing outside of var()
    • Chromium (Edge, Whale): = works (Free Sans)
  • var(--notosans, var(--dejavusans)) = works (Noto)
  • var(--notosans2, var(--dejavusans)) = works (DejaVu)
  • var(--notosans2, var(--dejavusans, 'freesans-test-one')) = works (DejaVu)
  • var(--notosans2, var(--dejavusans2, 'freesans-test-one')) = works (Free Sans)
  • var(--notosans2, var(--dejavusans2, 'freesans-test-one', 'notosans-test-one')) = fails, should be 1st fallback of 2nd var()

Interesting differences

This format works differently when used in font and color properties:

  • var(--notosans2, 'freesans-test-one', 'dejavu-sans-test-one') = works (Free Sans)
  • var(--red2, green, blue) - should be green, as per W3C
